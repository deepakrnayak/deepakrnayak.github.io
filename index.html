I am Currently working as an Assistant Professor at MNIT Jaipur, working with Department of computer science and engineering. Before joining MNIT Jaipur, I worked as an Assitant Professor at Department of computer engineering, SVNIT Surat, India and a Post-Doctoral fellow at IIITDM-Kancheepuram, chennai.

My current research interests mainly focus on medical image analysis and classification through machine learning, deep learning, pattern recognition and computer vision. I have executed one research project funded by SERB, DST, Govt. of India.

I received my Ph.d. in 2019 on computer science and engineering from National Institute of Rourkela under the guidance of Prof. Ratnakar Dash and Prof. Bansidhar Majhi. My thesis was focused towards designing improved pathological brain detection system using machine learning approaches. I received my master's degree in 2013 from CET Bhuvneshwar, India under the guidance of Prof. Prashanta Kumar Patra.

One of my papers was included in ESI Highly cited papers. I have also received 2020 CMES Young Research award from Tech Science Press for outstanding research in computer modeling and simulation. I also serve as an editorial board member of International Journal of Imaging Systems and Technology, Wiley since January 2021.
